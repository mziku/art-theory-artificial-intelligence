The Conscious Eye
Poly Kasda, Aigokeros Publ. (1988)


- Cognition - Art - Informatics

- Shift on Perspective: 'From seeing the world, seeing myself seeing the world'

- Art theory combined with concepts from computer science / artificial intelligence

Concepts:
* Cubism as an introspective state of mind
* Computer as a dada tool
* Minimal art and computational / cognitive processes
* Abstract Expressionism and Consciousness
